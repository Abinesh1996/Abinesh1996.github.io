About Me

I am a hydrologist specializing in climate-informed flood frequency analysis, hydrological extremes, and Bayesian inference. My research focuses on integrating large-scale climate drivers into flood quantile estimation under nonstationary climate conditions.

I completed my PhD at IIT Roorkee and work at the intersection of hydrology, climate science, statistics, and machine learning.

Research Interests

  • Climate-informed flood frequency analysis (CI-FFA)
  • Bayesian inference and uncertainty quantification
  • Large-scale climate drivers and hydrological extremes
  • Extreme precipitation analysis
  • Data-driven reservoir management
